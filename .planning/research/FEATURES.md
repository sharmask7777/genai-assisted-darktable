# Feature Research: Aesthetic Auditor & Variations

## Wildlife Aesthetics
- **Focus Areas:** Eye sharpness, background separation (bokeh), subject isolation.
- **Common Needs:** High-ISO denoising, shadow recovery (fur/feather detail), cropping for composition (rule of thirds/golden ratio).

## Landscape Aesthetics
- **Focus Areas:** Dynamic range (highlights/shadows), horizon leveling, color temperature (warm vs. cool balance).
- **Common Needs:** Graduated filters, local contrast enhancement, haze removal.

## Edit Variations
- **The "Naturalist":** Minimal intervention, accurate color reproduction, slight sharpening.
- **The "Dramatic":** High contrast, punchy colors, localized dodging/burning.
- **The "High-Key/Artsy":** Strategic overexposure or desaturation for creative effect.
