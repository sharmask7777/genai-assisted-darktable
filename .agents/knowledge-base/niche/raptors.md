# Raptors: Specialized Editing (Niche Research)

## 1. Talon and Beak Detail
Raptors require surgical sharpening on the talons and beak to emphasize their predatory nature.
- **Contrast Equalizer**: Raise the 3rd and 4th detail nodes (fine acutance) on the "luma" tab.
- **Diffuse or Sharpen**: Use "lens deblur | hard" with 60 iterations on the head only.

## 2. Background Management
Raptors are often shot against cluttered trees or sky.
- **Diffuse or Sharpen**: Use "surface blur" on the background to isolate the bird.
