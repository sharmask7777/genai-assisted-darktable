# Macro Photography Expert Workflow (2026)

## 1. Color and Saturation
Macro subjects (flowers/insects) often hit extreme color boundaries.
1. **AgX**: Superior at handling high-saturation hues compared to legacy filmic modules.
2. **Color Calibration**: Ensure CAT (Chromatic Adaptation) is used for accurate color constancy.

## 2. Micro-Texture
- **Contrast Equalizer**: Use the "Clarity" preset or raise fine-detail nodes to enhance insect carapaces.
- **Diffuse or Sharpen**: Use "sharpen" preset with high iterations on specific focal planes.

## 3. Noise
- **Chroma Denoise**: Prioritize this to keep flower petals smooth while preserving edge detail.
