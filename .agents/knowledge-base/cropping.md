# General Cropping & Composition Rules

## 1. Golden Rules
- **Rule of Thirds**: Place points of interest at the intersections of the grid.
- **Lead Room (Nose Room)**: Always provide more space in the direction the subject is looking or moving.
- **Horizon Leveling**: Ensure the horizon is perfectly level unless a creative "Dutch Angle" is intended.

## 2. Aspect Ratios
- **3:2**: Standard full-frame/APS-C ratio. Good for general use.
- **16:9**: Cinematic feel. Excellent for wide landscapes or "animal in environment" shots.
- **1:1**: Square crop. Great for centered, symmetrical subjects or social media.
- **4:5 / 5:7**: Standard portrait ratios. Good for tight subject focus.
