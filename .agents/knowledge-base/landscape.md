# Landscape Photography Expert Workflow (2026)

## 1. High Dynamic Range Management
AgX is the primary tool for managing high-contrast sky/foreground relationships.
1. **AgX Shoulder Power**: Increase to compress highlights naturally without hue shifts.
2. **Exposure**: Anchored to the most important texture (not necessarily the brightest highlight).

## 2. Haze and Depth
- **Diffuse or Sharpen**: Use the "dehaze" preset in a second instance of the module.
- **Masking**: Use a gradient mask to apply dehazing more strongly to the background/horizon.

## 3. Detail
- **Capture Sharpen**: Essential for resolving distant foliage or rock textures.
