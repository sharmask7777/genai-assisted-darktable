# Rough Idea

Implement a tiered research strategy for image processing in the `dt-ai` package:
- **Shallow Research**: Look at the local knowledge base only (Lightweight).
- **Deep Research**: Search the internet for new creative ideas and best practices.
- **Continuous Learning**: Every time deep research is performed, the findings must be used to iteratively build and refine the local knowledge base.
- **User Choice**: Give the customer the option to choose between Shallow or Deep research on every run to keep the workflow flexible.
